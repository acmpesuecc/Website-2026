Inauguration

Published on 2019-09-11

Authors: ACMPESUECC

ACM open-source

We, the members of ACM PES University EC Campus Student Chapter had our Inauguration ceremony on campus on the 11th of September.

Our main objective was to provide a general idea as to what ACM is, what ACM has to offer and other benefits of joining ACM. We provided information on how the attendees can join our chapter as well.

We were pleased to welcome Dr. Srinivas Padmanabhuni - "The Eminent Engineer and An AI Specialist" as our Chief Guest. A Former President of ACM, he is currently the Chief Technology Officer and cofounder at CityMandi, a B2B wholesale online grocery business.

The inaugural event was started with the lighting of lamp by Dr Srinivas, Dr Sandesh(HoD CSE Dept. and Faculty Coordinator ACM PESU-ECC) and the office bearers. This was followed by keynote speeches from Dr Srinivas and Dr Sandesh.

Speakers

  • Dr. Srinivas Padmanabhuni (Chief Guest)
  • Ankit Raj (Chair)
  • Kaushik Muralidharan (MC)

Venue: Seminar Hall - 1, PES University EC Campus
Duration: 2 hours(3:15PM - 5:15PM)
Number of attendees: 250
